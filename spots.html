My Favorite Spots

As a person who lives in New York City, I've grown to love spots all around the city. Here are just a few spots that I enjoy

  • First Evangelical Free Church: Every Sunday and summer, I would go here to worship, hang out, and play volleyball/basketball
  • Golden Corner Noodles: Since I have a Wenzhounese family, this restaurant has always been a go-to spot for us if you want the best noodles in the city
  • Blend Coffee + CoWork: Quiet coffee shop that is a good place to study, work, and relax
  • Dorcas Center: Fun place for me to play board games, do homework, or hang out with friends
  • Junior Golf Center: Place where I first learned golf. I come here often to practice with the coaches or some friends

Here's a list of the addresses of these spots if you ever want to visit them.
