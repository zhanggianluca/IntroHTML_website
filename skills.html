Skills

I don't see myself as a person who has skills to teach, but if I had to pick one skill to teach others it would be golf. I've been playing golf for nearly 10 years and many people underestimate how fun the sport is, so I want to teach others how to play it.

Over the years, I've played a lot of golf with other people. Some things I've done include...

  • Being the number two starter for the Brooklyn Tech Golf Team
  • Played at courses like Dyker Beach, Trump Links, Saxon Woods, and Richmond County
  • Being a member of the CityParks Academy Golf Team
  • Winning local tournaments at the CityParks Golf Center
  • Participating in the PSAL Golf Tournament

Here's a video of me practicing at a golf range

If you are ever interested in the sport of golf and want to learn a little bit more, I would gladly share some fun things about golf.
